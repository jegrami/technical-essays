## Hard Work Vs. Difficult Problem

Hard work is something that takes a lot of time and energy to do. Difficult problem is one that requires a lot of of thinking or some level of skill. cleaning your entire 5 bedroom flat is hard work. Writing a 5000-word essay on the house cleaning is a difficult problem. 

it becomes impossible to do difficult work when we are depressed because depression impairs out thinking and distracts focus. 

A problem is difficult it involves a lot of ambiguities and decision making that crucially affects the outcome. So how would you describe the work you do? Hard or difficult?
